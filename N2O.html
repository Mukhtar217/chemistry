Ionic Or Covalent

World Uses

N2O, is a colorless, almost odorless gas, that was first discovered in 1772 by the English scientist and clergyman Joseph Priestley. Priestley made N2O by heating ammonium nitrate in the presence of iron filings, and then passing the gas that came off (NO) through water to remove toxic by-products.
